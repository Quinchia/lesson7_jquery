Honey Garlic Salmon

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Salmon
  • 3 Tbsp butter
  • 3 Minced Garlic Cloves
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup of honey

Instructions

Place skillet on medium heat and allow butter to melt

When all of the better is melted, add minced garlic and sautee for 2 minutes

Add honey and stir until it becomes a thick glaze

Increase heat to medium-high, then place salmon on skillet

Heat oven to 400 F.

Flip so both sides of the salmon become golden and the outside appears cooked.

When oven is ready, place the skillet with the salmon in the oven for 10 minutes

Remove Salmon and pair with any veggies!
